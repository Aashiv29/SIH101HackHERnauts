SEASHELL SUM

IT'S TIME TO ADD AND FIND THE PEARLS OF THE OCEAN

A GAME THAT WOULD HELP BUILD THE NUMERICAL UNDERSTANDING BY ASSOCIATING THEM WITH A FACT IN SMALL KIDS THOSE WHO HAVE DYSCALCULIA.

Game Play

* The aim of this internet-based game is to determine a sum that equals 10.
* A fun fact and a random number between 0 to 10 will be given to you.
* You are now required to choose a number from 0 to 10 whose sum is equal to 10.
* It's an enjoyable and demanding approach to develop your talents and learn new things at the same time!!!

RULES

* The player will guess each number that they think adds up to 10 one at a time.
* As an example, if the player is shown the numbers 3 and then instructed to enter the number they think is the right guess (i.e. 7) for the sum to reach 10, they will have created a pair that is legitimate since 3+7-10.
*The player wins if the sum is 10 .
