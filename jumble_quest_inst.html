JUMBLE QUEST

IT'S TIME TO SLOVE SOME WORD MYSTERIES

A GAME THAT WOULD HELP BUILD WORD FORMATION AND UNDERSTANDING SKILL IN SMALL KIDS THOSE WHO HAVE DYSLEXCIA

Game Play

* Jumble Quest is a fun and educative single-player web game that will improve your knowledge of words and word-solving ability.
* In order to make understandable words in this game, you must untangle a collection of scrambled words.
* So, embark on Quest and see how many words you are able to decode if you're up for a brainteaser adventure and want to enhance your language skills.

RULES

* The ability of word unscrambling is at the heart of the game.
* You are given an anonymous word that has been completely jumbled by its letters.
* In order to score, your mission is to rearrange these letters into the correct English word.
