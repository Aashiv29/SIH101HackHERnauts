ADHD: Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is a diagnosis of a medical nature. People with ADHD have different patterns of brain activity and brain development, which influence their capacity for self-control and concentration. In an effort to encourage positive habits, we advise putting routines around homework, chores, and occasionally exercising

Dyslexia: Reading difficulty disorder

Dyslexia is a learning disability that causes people to have difficulty understanding word sounds and learning how to recognize letters and words. Be motivating, interacting with your child, and support them in learning. Enhance their sense of self-worth by finding interesting ways to impart knowledge.

Dyscalculia: Numerical processing disorder

A learning challenge termed dyscalculia affects one's ability to understand math and number-based knowledge. Some of the areas that these kids struggle with are visual processing, short-term memory, language, and math. We suggest encouraging creativity in your child, engaging them in arithmetic games and enjoyable homework.
