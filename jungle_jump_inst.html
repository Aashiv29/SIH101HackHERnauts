Game Play

* Enjoying the game of Jungle Jump is going to put your sense of direction and reactions to the test.
* The objective of this captivating game is to control a bar while preventing it from dropping the ball.
* So let's start the journey now!!!!!!!
* Jungle Jump offers straightforward gameplay that is easy to understand but difficult to master.
* You control a ball by simply sliding the bar left or right, and your goal is to prevent it from
crossing a bar on the screen.
* You can control the bar by using the left and right arrow keys on from your keyboard.
* Once the ball crosses the bar the game score resets and you LOSE STREAK!!!!!
